Memory Challenge

What is memory challenger ?

Memory Challenger is a game designed to strengthen your memory muscle. In today’s fast-paced world, many of us are stuck in the past or focused on the future, often losing the ability to stay present in the moment. This game helps improve your presence of mind by challenging your memory in fun, engaging ways.

How to Play Memory Challenger:

The game starts at a difficulty level with a 3-character length sequence. You have the option to play with Numbers, Alphabets, or Alphanumeric characters. Choose one from the options below.

If you successfully complete the challenge 3 times consecutively, the difficulty level increases by 1, extending the sequence length. However, if you fail 3 times in a row, the difficulty level drops to 1, giving you a chance to refocus and sharpen your memory.

Keep focus! Keep sharpening your brain.
